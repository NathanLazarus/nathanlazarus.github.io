## Works in progress  

**Dynamic R&D Investment** (with Mordecai Kurz and Kenneth Judd)
  <details>
  <summary>Summary</summary>
We study a dynamic investment game in which firms are differentiated by their R&D stocks and therefore productivities. When investing in R&D, firms face "success-breeds-success" probabilities, where accumulated successes improve their subsequent investment productivity. <br/>
